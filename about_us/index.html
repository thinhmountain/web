ABOUT FOUR PAWS

Our vision is a world where humans treat animals with respect, empathy and understanding!

The Problem

Many dogs, cats are surrendered by family who are unable to provide for them, or who simply do not want them any longer. Some are strays. And of course there are stories that I just don’t want to get into. Sometimes animals go through abuse that is completely unimaginable. But it’s never their fault.

Our Misson

FourPaws rescure stray dogs and cats, give treatment them a shelter. Then we will help match people with the perfect adoptable pets. But we don’t stop there, we also offer support and guidance after adoption to make sure pets and their people have long, happy lives together.
